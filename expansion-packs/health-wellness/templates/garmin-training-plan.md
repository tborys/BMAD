## Garmin Workout Files Included

### Easy Runs
- Easy_3mi.fit, Easy_4mi.fit, Easy_5mi.fit, etc.
- Heart rate guided with zones 1-2
- Audio prompts for pace checking

### Tempo Runs
- Tempo_20min.fit, Tempo_30min.fit, Tempo_40min.fit
- Structured warm-up, tempo portion, cool-down
- Lactate threshold pace guidance

### Interval Workouts
- 5K_Intervals.fit: 5 x 1K at 5K pace
- 10K_Intervals.fit: 4 x 1.5K at 10K pace
- VO2_Max.fit: 6 x 3min at 3K pace

### Long Runs
- Long_8mi.fit through Long_20mi.fit
- Heart rate guided with hydration reminders
- Negative split options for race simulation

## Upload Instructions
1. Download .fit files to computer
2. Connect Garmin device via USB
3. Copy files to GARMIN/NewFiles/ folder
4. Sync device with Garmin Connect
5. Workouts will appear in device training menu

## Progress Tracking
- Weekly time trials or race efforts
- Heart rate zone drift assessment
- Perceived exertion vs. actual pace
- Recovery metrics (HRV, resting HR)
- Training load and fitness trends
